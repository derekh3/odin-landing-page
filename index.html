Fruit is good for you

Fruit has essential vitamins and nutrients that are good for your health.

Here are some fruits

Apples are a great source of vitamin C and fiber. They also contain antioxidants like vitamin E.
Oranges are a good way to obtain vitamin C, folate, calcium and more.
Pineapples are loaded with vitamin B6, copper and thiamin, just to name a few.
Strawberries are rich in vitamin C, manganese, folate and potassium.
